A three-step handoff, visible from end to end.

Lasso keeps the screenshot, your comments, and the surrounding context together instead of scattering them across a prompt.

  1. 1 Frame the problem Press your shortcut and drag one deliberate rectangle.
  2. 2 Mark what matters Attach numbered pins or one note for the full capture.
  3. 3 Let the agent inspect it Claude Code, Codex, and Cursor read the capture through MCP.
